### SOD
- Definition：Service Order Delivery — core business entity representing a field work order that contractors execute using assigned materials and equipment. Central to the ERP's material tracking workflow from issue through completion.
- Aliases：service order、service delivery

### PAT
- Definition：Pre-Acceptance Test — verification process where field teams confirm completed work meets quality standards before final acceptance. Includes daily progress tracking and worker capacity monitoring.
- Aliases：pre-pat、pat verification

### OSP
- Definition：Outside Plant — telecommunications infrastructure including cables, poles, joints, and chambers installed in outdoor environments. The domain focus of the entire ERP system.
- Aliases：outside plant、osp operations

### OPMC
- Definition：Operator Management Control — organizational unit or area manager responsible for overseeing contractor teams and service order assignments within a specific geographic region.
- Aliases：opmc area、area manager

### RTOM
- Definition：Regional Technical Operations Manager — automated system that synchronizes service order data every 15 minutes, providing real-time visibility into field operations and contractor performance.
- Aliases：rtom sync、rtom data

### BOQ
- Definition：Bill of Quantities — automatically calculated material requirements (cables, poles, joints, chambers) generated from surveyed route geometry using telecom engineering formulas.
- Aliases：auto-boq、boq generation

### GRN
- Definition：Goods Receipt Note — formal acknowledgment of material delivery to stores, triggering inventory updates and procurement workflow progression.
- Aliases：goods receipt、grn entry

### MRN
- Definition：Material Requisition Note — formal request from field teams for materials needed to complete service orders, requiring approval workflow before issuance.
- Aliases：material requisition、mrn request

### RFC
- Definition：Request for Quotation — procurement document sent to suppliers to obtain pricing for materials required in purchase orders.
- Aliases：rfq、quotation request

### PO
- Definition：Purchase Order — formal contract with suppliers for procuring materials, generated from approved requisitions and quotations.
- Aliases：purchase order

### I-SHAMP
- Definition：Internal System for Hierarchy and Asset Management Planning — SLT's proprietary Excel format for importing service order data into the ERP system.
- Aliases：ishamp、slt ishamp

### Contractor Registration Portal
- Definition：Self-service web interface allowing contractors to submit registration documents (NIC, Business Registration, Police Report, Payment Slip) without administrative intervention.
- Aliases：contractor portal、self-registration

### Change Request
- Definition：Formal modification to project scope involving financial thresholds (<100K/500K) requiring multi-level approval workflow before implementation.
- Aliases：change request approval、scope change

### Retention
- Definition：Financial holdback percentage retained from contractor payments until project completion, tracked through payment voucher system.
- Aliases：retention amount、payment retention

### Nexus Agent
- Definition：AI-powered autonomous assistant that analyzes inventory levels, suggests stock transfers, and executes predefined actions like asset assignment and report generation.
- Aliases：nexus、ai agent、stock healer
